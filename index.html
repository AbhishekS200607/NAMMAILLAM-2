Tradition on a Plate,
Memories in
Every Bite.

In Malayalam, Namma Illam translates to 'Our Home'—and that is the soul of our kitchen. We are not just a vegetarian restaurant; we are a tribute to the warmth of a traditional South Indian home. Inspired by the heritage of our ancestors, we serve authentic, slow-cooked flavors that celebrate history, hospitality, and soulful dining.

A Taste of Tradition

From steaming idlis and crispy dosas to filter coffee that warms your soul, our menu celebrates the best of South Indian cuisine. Every dish is crafted with fresh ingredients, traditional methods, and a dash of innovation.
