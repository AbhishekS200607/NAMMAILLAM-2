Our Legacy

Namma Illam Means
'Our Home'

In Malayalam, Namma Illam translates to 'Our Home'�and that is the soul of our kitchen. We are not just a vegetarian restaurant; we are a tribute to the warmth of a traditional South Indian home.

Inspired by the heritage of our ancestors, we serve authentic, slow-cooked flavors that celebrate history, hospitality, and soulful dining. Every dish is prepared with the same honesty and care as a meal served to family.

Inspired by the Soil of Alappuzha

The spirit of Namma Illam is deeply rooted in the soil of Alappuzha. We draw inspiration from Raja Kesavadas, the visionary architect of modern Alleppey.

The Kolam Connection

You will notice the Kolam patterns in our decor. In our culture, these traditional designs symbolize welcome and auspiciousness. They are our way of greeting every guest who enters, honoring our cultural roots with every detail.

Meet the Founders

The Hands That Build
Our Home

Namma Illam is the culmination of a shared dream to preserve the warmth and honesty of a traditional South Indian kitchen. Our founders believe that food is the most powerful way to connect people, cultures, and generations.

Their vision was simple: to create a space that doesn't just serve meals, but offers a sanctuary of authentic taste and soulful hospitality. Every recipe we share is a tribute to the heritage that shaped them.

Their dream is to make Namma Illam a place where every guest we welcome is treated as an extension of our own family, and every bite carries the comfort of home.

Namma Illam Values

Rooted in tradition and driven by care, Namma Illam serves every meal with honesty, warmth, and uncompromising quality.
